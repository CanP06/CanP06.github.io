I'm a Chemical Engineering graduate building my career in production and process engineering. I enjoy thinking analytically, validating my assumptions with data, and bringing computational tools (Python, MATLAB, AutoCAD) into everyday engineering work. I believe working across disciplines produces more robust solutions, and I move forward open to learning and taking responsibility.

I also drive my professional development independently: I'm preparing my own lecture-note series on surface technologies and advanced manufacturing methods, and running a systematic study programme on production planning and refinery processes.

Hedefim, üretim ve proses mühendisliği alanında sahaya yakın ve teknik derinliği olan projelerde sorumluluk almak; uzun vadede üretim süreçlerini tasarlayan tarafta yer almak.
